# Peterna Hero Carousel — Gemini (Nano Banana / 2.5 Flash Image) prompts

**Direction (2026-05-10, Andre):** Six "real pet life moments" stills used as
the home-page hero background. They live behind the dark warm overlay + gold
radial tint — composition matters more than detail; warm tones, soft natural
light, and clean mid-frame negative space (so the inverted-white headline + gold
accent stay legible) matter most.

**Cadence (locked):** 6 slides · 6s each · 1.2s crossfade · ken-burns 1.00 → 1.06.
The carousel itself is in `src/components/HeroCarousel.tsx`; the slide list is
in `src/lib/hero-slides.ts`.

**All 6 outputs:**

- Aspect: 16:9 cinematic horizontal, native 2400×1350 minimum
- Style: warm filmic still, slight film grain, natural light only
- Color grade aligned to brand: cream `#F8F1E4`, soft gold `#C9A961`, blush
  `#E9D5C3`, sage `#8FA68E`, deep ink `#2A211B`
- Compose with breathing room — pets off-center / lower-third / right-third —
  so the upper-left third is calm enough for the headline to sit on top
- NO text, UI overlays, watermarks, logos, captions
- NO multiple animals, NO human faces in focus (silhouetted hands or feet OK)
- NO HDR, no over-saturation, no studio lighting, no commercial / cute framing
- NO close-up portraits — these read as a *moment from life*, not a hero shot

> A real Saturday. The dog is just being a dog.

NEGATIVE: studio lighting, indoor portrait, posed/staged, commercial pet-food
look, close-up face crop, multiple animals, human faces in focus, text, UI,
watermark, HDR, over-saturation, puppy.
